Hi. I am David,a tutor based in Vancouver.

I am creative, professional, and committed to instilling confidence and skills in the client.

I can help you develop all aspects of your English.

As an instructor / tutor I will collaborate to produce a focused yet fun and creative learning environment in which native and non-native clients can learn any and all aspects of English language communication.

IELTS

I will explain how each part of the exam is structured and provide tips on how to overcome some of the challenging and confusing elements of the test.

I WILL TELL YOU EXACTLY WHAT THE EXAMINER IS LOOKING FOR.

For example, for the speaking test, I explain the structure of the test and what the examiner is looking for, in terms of grammar, pronunciation and fluency.
Then, I lead the client through a ‘mock’ test, listening for, and correcting any problems. In terms of writing, I talk about how to analyze the writing tasks, and the particular vocabulary and grammar you need for Task 1.
I can provide online writing editing, as well.
